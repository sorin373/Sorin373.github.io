Different Breed

On 7th October 2022, me and other four colleagues signed up for a Game Jam, #Everygamercounts, organized by Goethe Institut. There were several other teams from Bucharest who took part in this contest, two of which were from George Cosbuc National Bilingual College, my team's place of study.

My team, OLOBOL, created the game entitled "Different Breed", which features multiple social problems from our everyday world. After being given the theme, Inclusion and Diversity, based on which every team had to create their own game, we came up with this great idea of using a dog as a playable character (and not a human being). This was because animals, unlike human beings, do not judge others based on their differences.

In spite of the fact that this was our first Game Jam, we succesfully submitted our game within the 48h time limit and mananged to win the tickets for the trip to Berlin. Furthermore, we are all looking forward to participating in future competitions related to game development.

This is the original Windows version of the game with which we competed in the Game Jam: download.zip/V1.

This is the updated version. We fixed the menu and other bugs: download.zip/V2.
